Travel Translation FAQs

Everything you need to know about using HyperMZT on your travels

Does HyperMZT work without internet?

Yes! With our offline mode, you can download language packs before your trip. This allows you to translate text and have basic conversations even without an internet connection.

How many languages does HyperMZT support?

HyperMZT supports over 50 languages, including all major travel destinations. We continuously add new languages based on traveler requests.

Can I use HyperMZT for real-time conversations?

Absolutely. Our conversation mode allows you to speak naturally and have your words translated instantly. The app handles turn-taking for smooth dialogues.

Is HyperMZT useful for reading signs and menus?

Yes, our camera translation feature is perfect for instantly translating menus, street signs, information boards, and other printed text.

How accurate are the translations?

HyperMZT achieves over 98% accuracy for common travel phrases and situations. For specialized vocabulary, we offer context-aware translations.
