Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about our legal translation platform.

How accurate is the translation for highly specialized legal terms?

Our Legal AI boasts an accuracy rate of over 98% for specialized legal terminology. It's trained on millions of legal documents, case law, and statutes from multiple jurisdictions to ensure contextually correct translations.

Is the platform compliant with legal data protection regulations?

Yes, absolutely. HyperMZT Legal AI adheres to GDPR, CCPA, and other major data protection laws. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we offer on-premise deployment options for maximum security.

Can it handle different legal systems (Common Law, Civil Law, etc.)?

Certainly. Our AI models are trained on legal texts from both Common Law and Civil Law systems, among others. It understands the conceptual differences and translates terms appropriately for the target legal framework.

What file formats are supported?

We support all major document formats including Word (.docx), PDF, Excel, PowerPoint, as well as common text formats. The original formatting, including tables and legal citations, is preserved during translation.
