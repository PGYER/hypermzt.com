Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about restaurant menu translation with HyperMZT

How accurately does HyperMZT translate food terms and ingredients?
HyperMZT achieves 98%+ accuracy for food and restaurant terminology with specialized culinary translation models. Our AI understands regional food variations, cooking methods, and dietary terms across 100+ languages.
Can customers with dietary restrictions find suitable dishes easily?
Yes! Our smart filtering automatically highlights vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, and allergy-friendly options. Customers can set their preferences once and see personalized menu recommendations.
Does HyperMZT work with existing restaurant POS systems?
Absolutely! HyperMZT integrates seamlessly with popular POS systems like Square, Toast, Resy, and OpenTable. Orders placed through our app sync directly with your kitchen and payment processing.
How does the cultural food insight feature work?
When customers select their country of origin, HyperMZT suggests dishes similar to their homeland cuisine and explains unfamiliar ingredients using familiar comparisons. It's like having a cultural food ambassador.
Is there training required for restaurant staff?
No extensive training needed! HyperMZT is designed to be intuitive. We provide a 30-minute onboarding session and 24/7 support. Most restaurants are fully operational within one day of setup.
