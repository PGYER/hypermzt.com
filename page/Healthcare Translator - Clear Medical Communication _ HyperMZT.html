Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about our healthcare solution.

How accurate is the medical translation?

Our AI is trained on vast datasets of medical literature, case studies, and expert conversations, achieving over 99% accuracy for common medical terms and phrases. Context is always considered for the most precise translation.

Is it suitable for all medical specialties?

Yes, HyperMZT Healthcare covers a broad spectrum of medical specialties, from general practice to cardiology, oncology, and pediatrics. We continuously update our database to include niche terminology.

How does it ensure patient privacy?

We are fully compliant with HIPAA regulations. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Conversations and documents are not stored unless explicitly permitted by the user for quality improvement (which can be opted out of).

Can it translate medical documents?

Absolutely. Our platform supports the translation of various medical documents, including patient histories, discharge instructions, research papers, and pharmaceutical information, maintaining formatting and critical details.
