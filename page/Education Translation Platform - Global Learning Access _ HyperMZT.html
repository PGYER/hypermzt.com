Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about our education platform.

Is it suitable for all age groups?

Yes, HyperMZT is designed to be adaptable for K-12, higher education, and adult learning environments. The interface and features can be tailored to suit different age groups and learning levels.

How does it handle technical or scientific terms?

We have specialized translation models trained on vast academic corpora. This ensures high accuracy for technical, scientific, and subject-specific terminology, crucial for educational content.

Can it translate assessments and exams?

Absolutely. HyperMZT can translate quizzes, tests, and exam papers while maintaining the original formatting and meaning. This helps ensure fair and accurate evaluation for all students.

What platforms does it integrate with?

HyperMZT integrates with popular Learning Management Systems (LMS) like Moodle, Canvas, and Google Classroom. We also offer API access for custom integrations with your institution's systems.
