Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HyperMZT

Is there a free version available?

Yes! HyperMZT offers a free version that includes core translation features for text and voice. The premium version unlocks offline translation, document scanning, and professional translation features.

How many languages does HyperMZT support?

HyperMZT supports over 100 languages for text translation and 40+ languages for voice conversation. We continuously add new languages and dialects to serve our global community.

Does HyperMZT work without internet connection?

Yes! With premium, you can download language packs for offline use. Simply select the languages you need before your trip, and you'll have translation capabilities even without internet access.

How accurate is the translation?

HyperMZT uses advanced neural machine translation trained on billions of multilingual sentences. For most common languages, accuracy exceeds 95% in real-world tests. For specialized content, we offer domain-specific translation modes.

Is my data secure with HyperMZT?

Absolutely. We prioritize security and privacy. All translations are encrypted end-to-end, and we never store your personal conversations. You have complete control over your data.
