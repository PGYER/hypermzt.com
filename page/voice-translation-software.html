Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HyperMZT voice translation

How does HyperMZT preserve my voice characteristics?

Our proprietary VoiceDNA technology analyzes your speech patterns, tone, and emotional cues. When translating, it applies similar vocal characteristics to the output using advanced voice synthesis.

Can I use HyperMZT for professional meetings?

Absolutely. HyperMZT is used daily by thousands of professionals. The Enterprise version includes meeting mode which handles turn-taking and speaker identification for group conversations.

What languages are supported for voice translation?

We currently support over 50 languages with more added monthly. Popular languages include English, Spanish, Chinese (Mandarin), Arabic, French, German, Japanese, Russian, and Portuguese.

How accurate is the translation?

Our latest accuracy tests show 98.2% accuracy for general conversation. Industry-specific vocabulary modules are available for technical fields like medicine, law, and engineering.

Is my voice data secure?

We prioritize your privacy. Voice data is processed locally on your device when possible. Cloud-processed audio is encrypted end-to-end and never stored after translation.
