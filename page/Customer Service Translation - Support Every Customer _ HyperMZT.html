Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions about our customer service solution.

Which languages are supported?

HyperMZT supports over 100 languages and dialects, including all major world languages like Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and many more. New languages are added regularly.

Does it work with our existing helpdesk software?

Yes, HyperMZT integrates seamlessly with popular helpdesk platforms like Zendesk, Salesforce Service Cloud, Freshdesk, and others via our robust API or pre-built connectors.

How does the AI handle slang or industry jargon?

Our advanced AI models are trained on diverse datasets, including customer service conversations and industry-specific texts. It understands context and can accurately translate colloquialisms and technical terms relevant to your business.

Can it translate customer feedback and surveys?

Certainly. HyperMZT can translate incoming customer feedback, reviews, and survey responses into your team's preferred language, allowing you to understand and act on insights from your entire customer base.
